Northeast Wisconsin's much-awaited Pongal Celebration event is planned for 3rd February 2024 as an In-Person event.

Date & Time: 3rd February 2024 (Saturday) from 4pm to 10pm
Venue: Menasha High School Auditorium, 420 7th St, Menasha, WI 54952

Admission:
  • General: 20$
  • Child (3 to 12 years): 10$

The event is planned to start at 4pm with snacks and appetizers. The cultural program will begin at 5pm. Please mark your calendar, join the fun with your friends & family for a memorable celebration. Stay tuned for further communication.

If you wish to participate in the cultural events, Please enroll before Friday, 12th January by contacting our cultural coordinators Arun Kumar Loganathan (Email: jp.arun85@gmail.com, Phone: 920-544-2997) or Swaminathan Balakrishnan (Email: swaminathan.ba@gmail.com, Phone: 920-843-8645) with the following information:

  • Who is going to perform - Contact name, phone number, email
  • What is your performance - Dance/Singing/Speech/Recital/anything else
  • Is it a Team performance or an individual performance - If it is a team, then the team name
  • Total Duration - approximate total time of your performance

This upcoming event is organized by the Northeast Wisconsin Tamil Sangam (NEW Tamil Sangam) in collaboration with Indian and South Asian community members. Our goal has always been to bring our communities together, provide a platform to showcase everyone's talents and kids' performances. We are hoping that you will continue your support for this effort to keep our community engaged and working together.

Each vibrant generation of Tamils embraces the joyous responsibility of shaping the future of our community. The Northeast Wisconsin Tamil Community radiates enthusiasm, representing our shared commitment to this exciting journey ahead. We actively foster and energize our dynamic community through engaging outreach and spirited community planning.

Why Join Us?

  • Cultural Celebrations: Immerse yourself in the beauty of Tamil traditions through our festive cultural events, where dance, music, and art come together to create unforgettable experiences.
  • Community Engagement: Be a part of a supportive and welcoming community that values diversity. Forge meaningful connections with fellow Tamils in Northeast Wisconsin and beyond.
  • Educational Initiatives: Stay informed and enriched with our educational programs, workshops, and seminars that delve into various aspects of Tamil history, language, and contemporary issues.
  • Social Events: Join in the fun at our social gatherings, picnics, and outings. Whether it's a casual meetup or an exciting event, you'll always find a warm and friendly atmosphere.
  • Philanthropy and Outreach: Contribute to meaningful causes through our community outreach programs. Make a positive impact both locally and globally with the support of like-minded individuals.

Join us today and become a vital part of the Northeast Wisconsin Tamil Sangam! Together, let's celebrate our heritage, create lasting friendships, and build a stronger Tamil community in Northeast Wisconsin.
