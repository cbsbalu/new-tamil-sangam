Northeast Wisconsin's much-awaited Pongal Celebration is back after 2 years. The event is tentatively planned for 21st January 2023 as a Virtual Streaming event. 

What: Northeast Wisconsin Pongal 2023
Date: 21st January 2023 (Tentative)
Where: Virtual Streaming

Please mark your calendar, join the fun with your friends & family (across the globe) & stay tuned for further communication.

If you wish to participate in the cultural events, Please enroll before Wednesday, 28th December by contacting our cultural coordinators Arun Kumar Loganathan (Email: jp.arun85@gmail.com, Phone: 920-544-2997) or Swaminathan Balakrishnan (Email: swaminathan.ba@gmail.com, Phone: 920-843-8645) with the following information: 
  • Who is going to perform – Contact name, phone number, email
  • What is your performance – Dance/Singing/Speech/Recital/anything else
  • Is it a Team performance or an individual performance – If it is a team, then the team name
  • Total Duration – approximate total time of your performance
The goal of this event is to highlight our communities talents and provide a platform to showcase our kids' performances. We will get Pre-Recorded performances from you and stream them on YouTube/Facebook on 21st January 2023. Additionally, with your permission, we will be publishing your recordings to the Northeast Wisconsin Tamil Sangam Social Media Channels (YouTube & Facebook). 

If you need a venue to record your performance or any assistance with recording, you can reach out to us. More details will be shared in the coming weeks.

Why Virtual: With Covid still around and the Flu season being worse than ever, many expressed concerns about having a large gathering. Hence we decided to NOT have an in-person event but still look for ways to celebrate Pongal. Considering the safety of everybody, we are moving forward with organizing the event virtually. 

This upcoming event is organized by the newly formed Northeast Wisconsin Tamil Sangam (NEW Tamil Sangam) in collaboration with Indian and South Asian community members. We are hoping that you will continue your support for this effort to keep our community engaged and working together.
