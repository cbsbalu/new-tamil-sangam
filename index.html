Northeast Wisconsin's much-awaited Pongal Celebration event is planned for 3rd February 2024 as an In-Person event. 

What: Northeast Wisconsin Pongal 2024
Date/Time:  3rd February 2024 (Saturday) from 4 pm to 10 pm
Where: Menasha High School Auditorium, 420 7th St, Menasha, WI 54952
Admission: General: 20$
                     Child (3 to 12 years): 10$

The event is planned to start at 4 pm with snacks and appetizers. The cultural program will begin at 5 pm. Please mark your calendar, and join the fun with your friends & family for a memorable celebration. Stay tuned for further communication.

If you wish to participate in the cultural events, Please enroll before Friday, 12th January by contacting our cultural coordinators Arun Kumar Loganathan (Email: jp.arun85@gmail.com, Phone: 920-544-2997) or Swaminathan Balakrishnan (Email: swaminathan.ba@gmail.com, Phone: 920-843-8645) with the following information: 
  • Who is going to perform – Contact name, phone number, email
  • What is your performance – Dance/Singing/Speech/Recital/anything else
  • Is it a Team performance or an individual performance – If it is a team, then the team name
  • Total Duration – approximate total time of your performance

This upcoming event is organized by the Northeast Wisconsin Tamil Sangam (NEW Tamil Sangam) in collaboration with Indian and South Asian community members. Our goal has always been to bring our communities together and provide a platform to showcase everyone's talents and kids' performances. We are hoping that you will continue your support for this effort to keep our community engaged and working together.
