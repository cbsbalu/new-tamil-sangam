It is time to come together (virtually) and enjoy Pongal with our friends & family across the globe.

What: Northeast Wisconsin Pongal 2023
Date: 28th January 2023 @ 6:30 pm (CST)
Where: Virtual Streaming @ NEW Tamil Sangam Facebook group
 
Dance, Song, and Speech performances will mark this Pongal event as another memorable day for all of us. Mark your calendar and spread the news. The event will be streamed on Facebook on 28th January at 6:30 pm (CST). For viewing the event, visit the NEW Tamil Sangam Facebook group and go to the Events section. You need to "Join Group" to watch the live event. So please make sure to join before the event. More information will be shared on the NEW Tamil Sangam website.

This upcoming event is organized by the newly formed Northeast Wisconsin Tamil Sangam (NEW Tamil Sangam) in collaboration with Indian and South Asian community members. We are hoping that you will continue your support for this effort to keep our community engaged and working together.
